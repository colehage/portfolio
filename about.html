About Me

Hi, I'm Cole, a passionate Blazers fan and CS110 student. I created this page to share my love for the Portland Trail Blazers and to connect with fellow fans. Whether it's coding or cheering for the Blazers, I'm always excited to combine my interests! I picked the Blazers because I thought that there was so much to display on them. Since they are important, I wanted to display them in the best light as possible.

Feel free to reach out to me if you'd like to connect or collaborate on a project. Go Blazers!
