Welcome to the Ripcity!

The Portland Trail Blazers are one of the most historic franchises in the NBA. The Portland Trail Blazers were established in 1970 as part of a long effort by the city of Portland to bring an NBA franchise to the people in the city of roses. Look through our page to see why we are the best franchise to support in the NBA.

Why We Love the Blazers

1 NBA Championship

Despite only 1 championship that we hold, each win is celebrated because of the importance to the people of Portland that this team brings.

Damian Lillard

Damian Lillard is the single most important player to ever play for the Blazers. "Dame Time", his nickname, was given to Lillard as part of a historic game winning shot.

Moda Center

The Blazers play in the Moda Center which was originally named the Rose Quarter. The Moda Center represents Love, Spirit, Heartbreak, and Joy to Blazers fans alike.
